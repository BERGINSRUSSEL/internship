Volvo-Eicher Commercial Vehicles Limited

EICHER CENTER OF EXCELLENCE
Dept. of Mechanical Engineering (Automobile)

ABOUT THE PROGRAMMES

In a first of its kind venture for an engineering college in Kerala, Amal Jyothi College of Engineering has signed up an MoU with the globally renowned VE Commercial Vehicles Ltd and has started a center for Automobile Technology on Amal Jyothi campus. This Center is offering internship for engineering aspirants with the current technological trends in industry with hands-on experience in special tools.
Upon successful completion of the internship, the students are provided with the certificate from Eicher.







SIX-DAY INTERNSHIP PROGRAMME


Duration
1 week (6 days)

Slot available
May - August (New batch starts every week)

Course fees
Rs. 6000/- (Rupees Six Thousand Only)

Batch size
24 (Preferable)

Course structure
1 hr Theory & 5 hrs practical per day


SPLIT UP OF COURSE - EICHER


Day Work
One Basics of engine- engine dismantling, dismantling & assembly of cylinder head
Two Dismantling of engine (piston, connecting rod, crankshaft...) assembling back to the system
Three Dismantling of gearbox
Four Dismantling of clutch (FN), dismantling of differential unit (AN)
Five Dismantling of braking system and study of components
Six Dismantling of steering gearbox (FN), electrical wiring harness (AN)
